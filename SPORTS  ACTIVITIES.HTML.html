SPORTS ACTIVITIES AT EDDIE

Sports play a vital role in the holistic development of students, our schools provide a perfect platform for young athletes to explore their talents, learn valuable life skills, and build a strong foundation for their future.

It also aids in maintaining a healthy weight and reducing the risk of various health conditions such as obesity, cardiovascular diseases, and diabetes. By encouraging sports in our schools, sports teachers promote a healthy lifestyle and instill habits that can last a lifetime.

We have a number of sports activities ranging from Football, Volleyball, Netball, Rugby, Hockey etc


Sport

Description

Football

Team sport played with a spherical ball between two teams of eleven players.

Basketball

Team sport played on a rectangular court, where two teams of five players try to score points by shooting a ball through the opponent's hoop.

Volleyball

Team sport played on a court with a raised net, where two teams try to hit a ball over the net and score points by making it touch the ground on the opponent's side.

Athletics

Collection of sporting events that involve competitive running, jumping, throwing, and walking.

Badminton

Racket sport played with a shuttlecock across a net, either in singles or doubles matches.

Gymnastics

Sport that involves physical exercises and acrobatics, often performed on apparatus like bars, beams, and vaults.
